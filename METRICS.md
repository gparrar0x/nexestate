# Metricas — NexEstate

## North Star
**Landings activas generando leads** (combina adopcion + valor real)

## KPIs por fase

### Fase 1 (MVP)
| Metrica | Target 3 meses | Fuente |
|---------|----------------|--------|
| Orgs registradas | 10 | Supabase |
| CRMs conectados | 5+ | crm_connections |
| Landings publicadas | 50+ | landings table |
| Form submissions | 200+ | landing_submissions |
| Chat sessions | 100+ | chat_sessions |
| MRR | USD 500+ | MercadoPago |

### Fase 2 (Crecimiento)
| Metrica | Target 6 meses | Fuente |
|---------|----------------|--------|
| Orgs activas | 30 | monthly active |
| Videos generados | 100/mes | video service |
| Leads PLUS entregados | 500/mes | scored leads |
| MRR | USD 3,000+ | MercadoPago |
| NPS | >40 | survey |
| CAC | <USD 100 | marketing spend / new orgs |

## Metricas de producto
- Time to first landing (onboarding speed)
- Landings por org por mes (engagement)
- Conversion rate landing → submission
- Chat queries por usuario por semana
- Sync reliability (% syncs exitosos)
